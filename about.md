Hi, my name is **Lars Schütze**.

I am a researcher at Technische Universität Dresden persuing my PhD at the [Chair for Compiler Construction](https://cfaed.tu-dresden.de/ccc-about).
I research the efficient compilation and interpretation techniques for role-oriented programming languages within the DFG funded research training group [RoSI](https://wwwdb.inf.tu-dresden.de/grk/) (Role-based Software Infrastructures for continuous-context-sensitive Systems).
